DINING

Guests looking for a luxury dining restaurant can choose between Ziya, our contemporary Indian speciality restaurant, with an innovative menu designed by the Michelin-starred Chef Vineet Bhatia, our Italian restaurant: Vetro with its attached wine library: Enoteca, our international all day dining space: Fenix, the sunlit Champagne Lounge, well stocked Patisserie & Delicatessen or chic, late night Eau Bar.

Our sophisticated restaurants in Mumbai each have their own distinct flavour. Guests can dine on fine Indian, Italian, Japanese sushi or pan Asian dishes, while enjoying a fine wine, a hot or cold Japanese sake, Japanese whisky, Scottish malts, a champagne cocktail or high tea.

Classical music is played on the spectacular red piano in our atrium, every day, and live music fills the bar six nights a week.

We also offer specially curated dining experiences for couples at our beautiful poolside. Those interested in cooking can opt for a private masterclass with an Oberoi chef. Please contact The Oberoi, Mumbai for more information.
