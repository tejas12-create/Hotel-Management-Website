Our Rooms

Deluxe Room

Furnished with a queen size bed, a silk armchair and decorated in shades of ivory and pale jade, our Deluxe Rooms are refreshing, comfortable spaces that afford peace and tranquility.Deluxe Rooms covering an area of 333 square feet, are suitable for single occupant, business or leisure visitors to the city.

Each room features a smart, wooden desk with desk drawer plug points, a mother of pearl mini-bar, three telephones, ceiling mounted reading lamps and an iPod dock. There is a DVD player and 40" television in the bedroom, and a 17" television beside the bathtub.

The bathroom exudes luxury and style. Tiled in a creamy Botticino marble, it is fitted with a full length standalone bathtub, a separate shower cubicle with multi pressure settings, and offers guests soft, cotton bathrobes, fluffy towels and exclusive toiletries.

Luxury Room

Spread across 400 square feet (37 square metres), our Luxury Rooms are spacious accommodations, abundant with natural light that flows in through large windows. From the comfort of their room

Our Luxury Rooms feature a king size bed, a walk in wardrobe, a silk armchair, a coffee table and wooden writing desk. Reading lamps are sunk into the ceiling above the bed, plug points can be found in the bedside walls and desk drawers, and a 40" television and a DVD player rest upon a mother of pearl personal bar.

The luxurious bathroom is equipped with a full length, standalone bath and a chrome hand shower, a separate vari pressure shower and sunken wash basins. The floors and walls are tiled in creamy Botticino marble, glowing with spotlights and backlit mirrors. The bathroom also features a 17" bathside television.

PREMIER SUITE

Premier Suites are suitable for business or leisure visitors to Mumbai. Spanning 870 square feet (81 square metres), Premier Suites include a wooden floored master bedroom, an expansive living and dining space, a marble bathroom and a walk in wardrobe.

The epitome of luxury and convenience, this bathroom has a floor-to-ceiling glass wall that affords unparalleled views of the ocean. It is equipped with an automatic screen, should guests require privacy when bathing, and fitted with a standalone bath, a separate shower, sunken basins and an automatic WC.

Single Bedroom

The standard rooms with single bed are approx. 20 sqm and have a combinated living- and sleeping area. The rooms are also equipped with a Wi-Fi access, small fridge, flat screen TV, coffee and tea making facilities and a safe. Upon arrival to your room, you will also find a bottle of mineral water, daily newspaper and a bathrobe, with which you can explore the free spa area.
